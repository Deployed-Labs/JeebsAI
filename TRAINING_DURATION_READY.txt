WHAT WAS ADDED:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

⏱️ MINIMUM DURATION: 1 minute (60 seconds)
   • If cycle finishes too quickly, automatically continues
   • Keeps exploring new domains until 1 min is reached
   • Ensures substantial learning each cycle
   • Prevents wasted shallow cycles

⏱️ MAXIMUM DURATION: 5 minutes (300 seconds)
   • Hard stop - never runs longer
   • Gracefully exits at max time
   • Prevents runaway cycles
   • Time-bounded and predictable


HOW IT WORKS:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TRAINING CYCLE PHASES:

  Phase 1: Crawl Random Websites
    └─ Explores 2 random sites
    └─ Checks max time after each
    └─ Stops if >= 5 minutes

  Phase 2: Research Topics
    └─ Research up to 7 topics
    └─ Checks max time after each
    └─ Stops if >= 5 minutes

  Phase 3: Extended Exploration (if needed)
    └─ If cycle < 1 minute: Continue!
    └─ Randomly pick more sites
    └─ Keep crawling until:
       ├─ Reaches 1 minute minimum, OR
       └─ Reaches 5 minute maximum

  Phase 4: Finalize
    └─ Complete and report results


TIMING ALGORITHM:

  1. Start timer
  2. Do main work (websites + topics)
  3. Check: Have we hit 5 min?
     └─ YES: Stop now!
     └─ NO: Continue
  4. Check: Are we at least 1 min?
     └─ YES: Ready to finish
     └─ NO: Continue exploring
  5. Repeat 3-4 until done
  6. Finalize and report
